Welcome to Rockafairy!

Our Mission:

"...to provide anyone who desires the necessary means for creative expression!"

Rockafairy is a public benefit non-profit which serves the Rogue Valley by offering creative resources - a library of musical equipment and art supplies, artistic collaboration space, private rehearsal space, mentoring, lessons, and so much more!
